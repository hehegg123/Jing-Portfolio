About me

I believe in the transformative power of language to shape interactions by conveying both emotions and information effectively. In my research, I focus on designing In-Vehicle Intelligent Voice Assistants that intuitively adapt to user needs and communicate efficiently, effortlessly, and engagingly through natural human language. Through such human-centered designs, my goal is to enhance safety, usability, and the overall user experience in automated vehicles.
